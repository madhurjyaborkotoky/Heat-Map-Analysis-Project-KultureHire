## Welcome to the Prerequisite Program

### What to do
You are given a task to analyze a summarized data and find the day where the most sales happen in a pizza store.

## What you will achieve
You would have learnt about creating Heat maps using Conditional formatting in Excel and understood how heat maps help you visually understand patterns.


## What the output should look like.

1. The heat map should be of a 2 color scale.

2. The color for the highest value should be dark blue. 

3. The color for the lowest value should be white.
